About Viruses

Viruses are microscopic organisms that can cause various diseases in humans, animals, and plants. They are unique due to their inability to reproduce without a host cell.

Prevention Tips

  • Wash your hands regularly with soap and water.
  • Avoid close contact with sick individuals.
  • Get vaccinated when vaccines are available.
  • Maintain a healthy diet to support your immune system.
  • Disinfect surfaces regularly.
